What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a type of two-dimensional barcode that can store various kinds of information, such as text, URLs, contact details, and more. It can be easily scanned using a smartphone camera, providing a quick and convenient way to access digital information in the physical world.

Common Uses for QR Codes

  • Website Links: Direct users to your website, a specific landing page, or your social media profile.
  • Wi-Fi Access: Store your Wi-Fi network name and password, allowing guests to connect automatically by scanning the code.
  • Contact Information: Create a digital business card (vCard) that users can scan to instantly save your contact details.
  • Event Information: Link to event details, maps, or registration pages.
  • Menus and Payments: Commonly used in restaurants for contactless menus and payment processing.
